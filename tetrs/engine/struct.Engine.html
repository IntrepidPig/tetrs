Stores the internal engine details.

This is largely segmented into components EngineSettings, EngineInternal to reduce the overall complexity and namespace various features.

Most names are condensed a lot to provide shorter references.

Methods

impl Engine

fn update(&mut self)

The main update phase of the engine.

This handles DAS and all other internal complications based on the current controller state only.

Each call to update is expected to take place in ~mspt ms. It is up to the caller to manage the update lengths appropriately.
