Handles building of more complicated fields than can be constructed with new itself.

More options may be added in the future whilst keeping backwards-compatibility.

Examples

use tetrs::field::{Field, FieldBuilder};

// Constructs a field with a width of 12 and height of 30
let field = Field::new().width(12).height(30);
